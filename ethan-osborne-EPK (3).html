As Seen on KFDM — ARC Southeast Texas

Born in Louisiana and raised in the heart of Southeast Texas, Ethan Osborne carries his roots into every note he plays. His music is a heartfelt blend of Southern grit and genuine Texas warmth — a true reflection of the life he's lived and the community that shaped him.

Ethan's connection to his fans runs as deep as his love for family and the people closest to him. Every song he writes is a tribute to those who support him, making him not just an artist, but a storyteller for his community.

Quickly gaining fans throughout Southeast Texas with regional media features and a growing live presence, Ethan Osborne's music is as real as it gets — resonating with anyone who values authenticity and heart.

Music that reflects real experiences — authentic, warm, and built for connection.
